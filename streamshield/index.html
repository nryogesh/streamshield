Installation Steps

Get StreamShield running in under 2 minutes

1

Click the Download button and save the zip

Save the file to your Downloads folder.

2

Unzip it

You will get a folder called streamshield_fixed

3

Open Chrome Extensions

Go to chrome://extensions in your address bar.

4

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle the Developer Mode switch in the top right corner.

5

Click "Load unpacked"

This button appears after enabling Developer Mode.

6

Select the folder

Choose the streamshield_fixed folder (not the outer zip folder).

7

Pin and launch

Pin StreamShield from the extensions menu and click it to open.
